INTRODUCTION

Noida, in the recent years has emerged as one of the most developed Tech cities in India and is well known for its lush greeneries, highly developed infrastructure, world class amenities and connectivity all around with Delhi/NCR.

Introduction and implementation of new policies by State/Central Govt. has tremendously helped in the development of infrastructure and improvement of connectivity across Noida, which has attracted many Indian and international companies to establish their businesses here. The Indian and MNCs have also found prospects of maximum productivity due to conducive environment and other facilities available in Noida.

Amongst all, Sector-59 and Sector-63 are the prominent sectors wherein many ultra modern IT/ ITES buildings exist and housed by numerous Indian and Multi National Companies.

The ultra modern looks of buildings are also a factor of attraction for companies from all over India. With corporate look buildings, ample parking spaces, metro connectivity, these sectors have now become a preferred location for the entrepreneurs who want to set up their businesses in Noida. Sector-59 and Sector-63, Noida are surrounded by numerous residential societies, reputed nearby schools, multi specialty hospitals, restaurants, banks etc. which makes it a promising location for all. In a short span of time these sectors have emerged as a cost effective official hub in comparison with Delhi and Gurgaon.
